An interactive example of a theorem from the upcoming paper ``Webification of plane partitions" (2025+), Ashleigh Adams and Jessica Striker.

Instructions: Drag the big web (on the left) to rotate both webs.

Description: The SL_4-web on the left is in bijection with the fundamental domain of a totally symmetric plane partition. One can map the web on the left to the SL_2-web on the right (Adams, Striker 2025+). Underneath both webs is the lattice word of the web. By rotating the webs, we see how rotaiton on the webs and promotion on the words match.
